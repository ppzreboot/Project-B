Privacy Policy

Extension: Prob
Last updated: 2026-04-02

Summary

  • No account required. The extension does not create user accounts.
  • No analytics. The extension does not include analytics or tracking.
  • No selling/sharing. The extension does not sell or share personal data.
  • Local-only settings. Voice and speech rate preferences are stored locally via chrome.storage.local.
  • No remote code. The extension package does not load or execute code from remote servers.

What the extension does

Prob reads aloud text that you select on a webpage using the browser’s built-in speech synthesis (Text-to-Speech) capability. Reading is triggered only when you use the extension’s command (keyboard shortcut).

Information we collect

We do not collect personal data.

The extension does not collect, transmit, or store browsing history, page content, or personally identifiable information.

Selected text

When you trigger the “read selection” command, the extension accesses only the text you have currently selected in the active webpage so it can be spoken aloud.

  • Not transmitted: selected text is not sent to any server.
  • Not stored: selected text is not permanently stored by the extension.

Storage

The extension uses chrome.storage.local to store user preferences locally on your device:

  • Voice: which speech synthesis voice you selected.
  • Rate: the speech rate you selected.

This data stays on your device and is used only to keep your preferences between sessions. It is not shared with third parties.

Permissions we use and why

  • storage: to save your voice/rate preferences locally.
  • Host permissions (http://*/*, https://*/*): to run the content script on the pages you visit so the extension can access your current text selection when you trigger the read command. The extension does not automatically read or transmit page data.

Third-party services

The extension does not use third-party services to process your data. Speech playback uses the browser/OS speech synthesis voices available on your device.

Children’s privacy

The extension is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact the developer via the support channel listed on the Chrome Web Store listing.

隐私政策（中文）

扩展：Prob
更新时间：2026-04-02

要点

  • 不需要账号：扩展不创建用户账号。
  • 不做统计/追踪：扩展不包含分析/埋点/追踪。
  • 不出售/不共享：扩展不会出售或共享个人信息。
  • 仅本地保存设置：语音与语速偏好使用 chrome.storage.local 存在本机。
  • 不使用远程代码：扩展不会从远端服务器加载或执行代码。

扩展做什么

Prob 会在你选中网页文字后，通过浏览器内置的语音合成（TTS）朗读。只有当你手动触发扩展的朗读命令（快捷键）时，才会开始朗读。

我们收集哪些信息

我们不收集个人信息。

扩展不会收集、上传或保存你的浏览历史、网页内容、或可用于识别个人身份的信息。

关于“选中文本”

当你触发“朗读选中文本”命令时，扩展仅访问当前页面上你选中的那段文字，用于朗读。

  • 不会上传：选中文本不会发送到任何服务器。
  • 不会长期存储：选中文本不会被扩展永久保存。

本地存储（storage）

扩展使用 chrome.storage.local 在本机保存以下偏好设置：

  • 语音（Voice）：你选择的朗读声音。
  • 语速（Rate）：你设置的朗读速度。

这些数据仅用于在下次使用时保持设置，不会与第三方共享。

我们使用的权限及原因

  • storage：用于保存语音/语速偏好到本机。
  • 站点权限（http://*/*、https://*/*）：用于在你访问的网页上运行内容脚本，以便在你手动触发朗读时读取你当前选中的文字。扩展不会自动读取或上传网页内容。

第三方服务

扩展不使用第三方服务处理你的数据。朗读使用的是你设备/浏览器提供的系统语音能力与语音库。

联系我们

如果你对本隐私政策有疑问，请通过 Chrome Web Store 的扩展详情页中提供的支持渠道联系开发者。
